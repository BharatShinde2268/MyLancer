How does freelancing work?

Freelance workers are typically independent contractors who offer paid services on top of their existing jobs. In today’s evolving workforce, freelancers no longer need to rely on outdated models like full-time employment to earn a living. MayLancer makes it easy for anyone with an online presence to find work as a freelancer. We connect businesses that want to hire freelancers directly with those seeking freelancing opportunities.
Our platform allows freelancers to set their own rates and choose the projects they want to complete. As a result, we have seen tremendous growth over the past few years.
